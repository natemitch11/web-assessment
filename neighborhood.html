Where I grew up

Hidden in the forest, a ways away from the sea lies where Straw Hat Nate grew up. There was always something do do in this village hidden in the leaves. Want to hike? check out the great stone faces of the Hokage. Want to fish? there are a plethora of rivers in the forest (try and steer clear of the waterfall though, theres always people there trying to stop the water with their chakra). Moral of the story is that no matter what it is you want to do, we got it for you.

Perhaps the best thing about the village is the food! There are quite a few places that Nate frequents often. He enjoys local cuisine as well as trying flavors from other places. Click Below to get a recommendation from Nate!
